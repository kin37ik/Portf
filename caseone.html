Australia Post

UX/UI Design / Research / Wireframing / Prototyping

2023

 

Background

I started this project inspired to create a better solution after experiencing my own problems with Australia Post, i wanted to examine ways to improve the service and make it more friendly to a wider range of people that use the service. Through research, i discovered that the problem in the first place is the website being far too cluttered with far too many options, making it difficult to find what one requires.

 

Goals

Redesigning the Australia Post website to be more user friendly and minimizing the myriad of links and options to reduce confusion amongst users.

 

Problem

The amount of links and pages for a majority of information is causing customers to become frustrated, lost and finding the information they require. Part of the problem is the overwhelming options and it being time consuming looking for what they need.

 

My Impact

For this project i validated the need to re-design the website and leveraged data from the research to create a condensed, comprehensible website service catering to the needs of the user.

Research Synopsis

I was surprised to learn how many users view the website for information regarding legal documents (Tax File Numbers, Police Check ect) and insurance, usually browsing longer than necesarry for what information they need. This led me to discover that what would help the users get onto the right information, would be to condense it down into easily digestible content.

Pivot

The insights gained from user testing and research gave me confidence that a redesign with simplified information and less links would address the issue at large.

Conclusion

While working on this project i learned that research and testing is really paramount to working towards the final product that with the best outcome for as many users as possible. Of course its not possible to 100% satisfy everyones wants and needs but even a 10% improvement is much welcome. I learned to not get too hung up on setting the design out in a specific way and to be fleixble in the ways of solving the problem. I beleive that slimming down the myriad of options and condensing the information is fixing the problem at the core.
